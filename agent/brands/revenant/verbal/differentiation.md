# revenant — differentiation

## vs. alpha newsletters / signal services

| Them | Revenant |
|---|---|
| Sells signals | Sells the apparatus that produces signals |
| Reader is dependent | Reader owns the output |
| Edge degrades as subscribers act on same signal | Each user runs their own sweep against their own constraints |
| Calibration is hidden | Calibration is the headline |
| Trust = testimonials | Trust = reproducible plot |

The core difference: **we don't sell the fish; we ship the rod, the boat, the sonar, and the regulatory framework**. Their model breaks when their list grows. Ours strengthens.

## vs. AI hedge funds / "quant for LPs"

| Them | Revenant |
|---|---|
| Raises capital from LPs | Does not raise capital |
| Reader is an allocator | Reader is the practitioner |
| The fund IS the product | The lab is evidence; the tools are the product |
| Pitches Sharpe at the LP | Publishes calibration at the practitioner |
| Talks to capital allocators | Talks to working quants |
| Sales motion: warm intros, capacity calls | Distribution: ship to Discord, let plots travel |

**The trap to avoid:** drifting into LP framing because LP money is bigger. The brand only works if the reader believes we are not chasing their capital.

## vs. traditional quant funds (e.g. mid-tier systematic shops)

| Them | Revenant |
|---|---|
| Infrastructure is internal IP | Infrastructure is shipped as product |
| Hires the cohort | Sells to the cohort |
| Edge = secrecy | Edge = the lifecycle vocabulary catching on |
| Quarterly LP reports | Daily live calibration |
| Hires from MIT / Princeton | Hires from the Discord |

Not competing on returns. Competing on **whose loop the working quant runs inside of**.

## vs. retail trading platforms (e.g. retail-focused systematic SaaS)

| Them | Revenant |
|---|---|
| Optimized for low technical floor | Optimized for high technical ceiling |
| "Backtest your TradingView strategy" altitude | "Cost-efficient CMA-ES across venues" altitude |
| Marketing aimed at curiosity | Marketing aimed at peers |
| Won by gamified UX | Won by honest fees + accurate calibration |

If a retail trader stumbles in and is confused, that is a positive signal. **The brand should feel uninviting to the wrong reader and obvious to the right one.**

## vs. Lore (sister brand)

| Lore | Revenant |
|---|---|
| B2B2C — partner distribution | Direct to practitioner |
| Reader = product team | Reader = working quant |
| Wraps LTPs as consumer products | Ships systematic infrastructure |
| Voice = institutional restraint | Voice = peer at the bar |
| Hero color = parchment + lime | Hero color = [Phase 0 — pending inspo] |
| Hero type = Lock Serif | Hero type = [Phase 0 — pending inspo] |

Same parent, same craft, same care for verifiability. Adjacent — not overlapping — surfaces.
