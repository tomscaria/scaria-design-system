# revenant — positioning

## What it is

A research lab that ships tools for the hobbyist and pro-am quant cohort. The tools let someone serious about systematic trading run the loop a top fund runs — model fit, sweep, backtest, paper-trade, promote — without having to build the infra themselves.

The lab does its own research in public. The tools are how others borrow that infrastructure.

## What it is not

- **Not an AI hedge fund.** We do not raise capital, we do not run a fund as the product. The lab trades its own strategies; that's evidence, not the offer.
- **Not an alpha newsletter.** We don't sell signals. We sell the apparatus that produces signals so the user owns the output.
- **Not a discount Bloomberg.** We're not aiming at the institutional desk. The institutional motion is Lore.
- **Not a managed service.** No "talk to sales," no demos, no implementation calls. Open the tool, run the sweep.

## Why it's distinct from Lore

Lore distributes wealth infrastructure to consumer apps via partners. The reader is a product team integrating LTPs.

Revenant distributes quant infrastructure to working quants direct. The reader is the person running the model.

Same parent (Scaria). Same craft (verifiable onchain finance). Different audiences, different distribution shapes, different voices.

## The frame

> "What if the apparatus a top systematic shop runs internally was something a serious solo quant could just open?"

Not "democratize quant." Not "AI for everyone." The frame is **specific tools shipped to a specific cohort that already exists** — the cohort just hasn't had infrastructure at this level before.

## Distribution shape

- **rswarm.ai** — public-facing marketing surface. Calibration plots, research notes, lifecycle dashboard, sweep cost calculator.
- **swarm-fund-mvp** — the tool itself. Where the loop runs.
- **Discord** — primary community channel. Reference users live there.
- **GitHub** — open-source primitives where it makes sense; sweep templates, indicator libraries.

No paid acquisition. No sales team. Distribution is the work being good enough that the reference cohort recommends it.

## Proof shape

- Live calibration plots updated as Canaries run.
- Lifecycle dashboard showing Birth/Canary/Apex/Revenant counts and transitions.
- Public research notes that show their work — failed sweeps included.
- Bench transparency — strategies that got retired and why.

The proof is **the artifact, not the testimonial**. Quant readers trust plots; they don't trust quotes.
